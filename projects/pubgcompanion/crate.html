PioneerCrate

Pioneer Crates contain semi-random gear for players. They are acquired with Battleground Points (BP), which are given every time a round has ended. The amount of BP earned depends on how far one has managed to survive, as well as number of kills and shots which hit another player. After each crate has been opened, the price of the next crate increases. The crates reset every Sunday at 8pm EST.

Pioneer Crate Cost

After opening 6 boxes, all the next will cost 7000 BP (BattlePoints).
