Hazard Protection Exosuit Environmental Shielding

Basic environmental shielding.

Protection from specific planetary conditions can be increased through installation of Hazard Protection units.

Device is charged by Oxide elements.

Operates automatically once constructed within user's exosuit inventory.

Additional Info:

The Hazard Protection starts working when:

The temperature exceeds 50°C

The temperature drops below -20 °C

The Toxicity level exceeds 5 Tox

The Radiation level exceeds 5 Rad

Recharge with:
Zinc x40100% Titanium x40100% Shielding Shard x1100%
